About Me

I am a full stack software engineer who is recently graduated from Hackbright Academy. Before Hackbright, I studied both design in University of Arts London and economics in Istanbul University. Prior to that I worked as an Operations and Chartering Deputy Manager in tanker shipping business, focussing on voyage calculations, operations, and chartering negotiations. After working for several years, I realized that the key points of my job were problem-solving and database building. After moving to South Bay, I was quickly inspired by the potential of programming and began to take online programming courses. At this point I decided to take my passion one step further by attending Hackbright. After Hackbright, I am excited to combine my design and creativity skills with my engineering skills.

Projects

GoVisEat

GoViseat is a webapp where users can make a search to choose where to eat in a specific city for a time period, save these recommended places and even search for other users to see their saved trips.

You can visit the GitHub link of the project GoVisEat for more information.

Memory Game

This is a browser-based card matching game. A user wins once all cards have successfully been matched. It has sixteen cards which contains a pair with different symbols on one side. User has to match cards which are arranged randomly, with identical face.

You can visit the GitHub link of the project Memory Game for more information.
